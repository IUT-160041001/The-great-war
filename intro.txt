The protagonist Alphonse was once an orphan. He grew up in a poor neighborhood where money and food were scarce.
However after crossing all the odds he became and apprentice of a knight. Going through rigorous and painstalking amount 
of training he becomes a soldier ready to defend his nation.
He is currently fighting a war between the two great nations Bavaria and Clover.
As a citizen of Bavaria he is enlisted as a soldier and prepares himself for the battle.
A horn is blown and every soldier gathers in front of the castle.
The commander, a man with strength and tactics calls his soldier.
"Soldiers, today you have the honour to defend your nation.
Do what you think is right and just for the nation.
You have the option to be a spearman,archer and swordsman.
Choose carefully."
